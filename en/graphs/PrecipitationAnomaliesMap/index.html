Interactive map: Precipitation anomalies in Southern Spain

In this page you will find an interactive map with several raster layers.

In the upper right corner you can select between two different layers: "Current year anomalies" and "Current water year anomalies"

The layer "Current year anomalies" includes a raster layer with interpolated anomalies of accumulated precipitation in the current year using the 2001-2021 climate normal as reference period. In the other hand, in the "Current water year anomalies" you will find interpolated data of anomalies of accumulated precipitation on the current water wear using the same reference period that in the "Current year anomalies" layer. A water year starts on September 1st and finishes on August 31st and it is used for agriculture purposes instead of "regular" years.
